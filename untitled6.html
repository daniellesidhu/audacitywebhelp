Generators

Generators create new audio in stereo tracks.

Table 1. Generators Index
Generator Function
Chirp Generates four different types of tone waveform
DTMF Tones Generates dual-tone multi-frequency tones
Noise Generates white noise
Silence Generates zero amplitude audio
Pluck Generates a synthesized pluck tone
Tone Generates sine, square, or sawtooth waveforms
